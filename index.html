ROGER HALLAWAY

If your dreams wrote slow jams, they'd sound like this

Roger Hallaway is a unique talent. His sound combines classic analog sounds of the past with the clarity of modern pop music. This results in a product that is fresh yet tinged with nostalgia.

Inspired by auteurs of the past, Roger is highly involved in all stages of his artistic output. He writes all of his own material, designs all of his own album covers, and directs all of his own music videos.

And his talents aren't just simply restricted to the studio environment. While performing live, Roger delivers an positively electrifying act while being supported by an elite group of musicians. His musical vision has no place for backing tracks or stale arrangements: each performance is guaranteed to be a undeniably unique and encompassing experience.

Roger currently resides in Los Angeles, California.
